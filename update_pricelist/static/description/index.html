💲 Update Pricelist

Update the prices of many products on a pricelist at once — by a fixed amount or by a percentage of their current price — without editing each pricelist line by hand.

✨ Key Features

  • Bulk update wizard launched from the Product Variants, Product Templates and Pricelist Items lists.
  • Increase prices by a fixed amount or a percentage of the current price.
  • Creates new, time-stamped pricelist lines and can optionally close the previous open lines so history is preserved.
  • Warns when selected products have no current price on the pricelist (a percentage increase would be computed from a base of 0).
  • Dedicated Sales → Pricelist Lines view showing latest purchase price, supplier, profit percentage and validity dates.

🚀 Usage

  • Open Sales → Products → Product Variants (or Pricelist Lines).
  • Select the products/lines to update and click Update Pricelist.
  • Choose the pricelist, the update type (Fixed / Percentage) and the amount.
  • Tick Close Old Pricelists to end-date the previous lines, then Submit.

✅ Compatibility

  • Odoo version: 18.0
  • Editions: Community and Enterprise
  • Hosting: Odoo.sh, On-Premise and Odoo Online (SaaS)

📦 Installation

  • Copy the module to your Odoo addons directory.
  • Update the app list.
  • Install the module from the Apps menu.
